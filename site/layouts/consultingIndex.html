Services

I shape each engagement around the client. These are the recurring service types I can provide.

Custom development I can help with complex frontend work, design-system-adjacent implementation, table-heavy interfaces, and the backend-facing side of frontend architecture. Workshops I run hands-on workshops on topics such as webpack, React, Deno, and Qwik. I prefer [predesigned katas](/workshops/) so attendees can work at different levels and still make concrete progress. Conference organizing I have run conferences since 2017, including [React Finland](https://react-finland.fi/), [GraphQL Finland](https://graphql-finland.fi/), and [Future Frontend](https://futurefrontend.com/). I can help with concept, speaker work, marketing, and event direction. Research My doctoral work has given me practical experience with scientific publishing, surveys, empirical research, and qualitative and quantitative methods. [See my research outputs](/research/) for the academic side. Cleanup I can review technical debt, produce prioritized improvement points, or dig into implementation details such as modernizing a webpack setup or moving a project toward a simpler toolchain. Coaching For individuals, I can provide focused weekly or occasional coaching around JavaScript, web development, technical writing, and professional growth topics.
